How to record data?

Sensors and equipment required:
  • ZED stereo camera
  • Camera vaccum mount
  • Multimeter
  • Exide battery - (7V, 18Ah)
  • US Global Sat GPS - BU353-S4
  • Intel NUC
  • 6-48V automotive power supply for NUC
  • A laptop to communicate with NUC
  • A phone with hotspot to act as a communication bridge between laptop and Intel NUC
Step1: Before setting up
  • Fully charge the battery
  • After full charge, connect the DCDC power supplier to battery
    • Make sure you have connected +ve(RED) to positive terminal of the battery and repeat same for -ve(BLACK) terminal. Do not connect in reverse, very important
    • Set the multimeter in DC Volts, and test the output from DCDC power supply. It should be around 19V. Minor fluctuations are okay.
    • In case of any other voltage, do not proceed as Intel NUC power board doesn't support
Step2: Physical setup
  • Mount the camera on the car using vaccum mount
    • Make sure you place the camera at the same position
  • Place the battery in a fixed place for an uninterrupted power supply
  • Place the Intel NUC on the car's dashboard
    • Connect the DCDC power supply
    • Connect the ZED camera in one of the four USB 3.0 ports
  • Switch on the Intel NUC and Laptop and make sure you connect them to the same wifi hotspot

Step3: Technical setup

1.SSH to the Intel NUC using your laptop and login to the NUC system remotely 

Varuns-MacBook-Pro:~ VarunPerla$ ssh arlcvit@172.20.10.**
Password:

2.After logging your terminal show the following: 

arlcvit@arlcvit-NUC7i7DNHE:~$

3.Change the current directory to catkin workspace 

arlcvit@arlcvit-NUC7i7DNHE:~$ cd catkin_ws/

4.Source the setup.bash file 

arlcvit@arlcvit-NUC7i7DNHE:~/catkin_ws$ source devel/setup.bash

5.Launch the ZED_CPU_ROS to collect the camera 

arlcvit@arlcvit-NUC7i7DNHE:~/catkin_ws$ roslaunch zed_cpu_ros zed_cpu_ros.launch

6. Now, we are almost ready to capture the data. But before that, lets check what all ROS topics are being published. To check the list - Open a new terminal in your laptop and login through SSH using the same process decribed above. 

arlcvit@arlcvit-NUC7i7DNHE:~$ rostopic list

This will show you all the ROS topics being published. It should look something like this and so on.

arlcvit@arlcvit-NUC7i7DNHE:~$ 
/camera/left/image_raw 
/camera/left/image_raw/compressed
/camera/right/image_raw 
/camera/right/image_raw/compressed
.....

7. Go to the desired folder where you want to store the recording and type this command 

arlcvit@arlcvit-NUC7i7DNHE:~$ rosbag record /camera/left/image_raw/compressed

8. After you have completed the drive and wanted to stop recording, Press Control+C together

9. There are two terminals open in your laptop, you have already stopped recording in one terminal by presseing Control+C together. Now do the same in other terminal also
